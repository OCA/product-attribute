================
Unit of Measures
================

This module adds missing unit of measures and their categories:

* Energy / Heat: calorie, kilocalorie, kilojoule
* Metric Volume
* Time

Known issues / Roadmap
======================

* Add other unit of measures and categories based on the demand

Bug Tracker
